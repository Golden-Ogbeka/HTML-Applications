Who is Jesus to you

This website is all about Jesus because all things in this world focuses on Him.
He is the Alpha and the Omega. The Beginning and the End. He know your end
from your beginning. He is Awesome. He is Super. There are not enough words to describe
Him because He is just too much to describe. He is my Savior, My Redeemer, my Provider
my Sustainer, he is my all in all. My whole life is centered around Him. My whole life depends
on Him. Without Him, I can do nothing.

So what stops me from praising Him? What stops me from worshipping Him? What stops me
from communicating with Him when I know that He is the Author and Finisher of my faith?

Please praise Him with me.
